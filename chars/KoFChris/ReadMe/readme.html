Description

This archive contains the MUGEN conversion of the character named Duo Lon.

The sprites of this Chris come from The King of Fighters '97, '98, 2002, 2002 UM, and some edits. The voice sets are taken from The King of Fighters '97, '98, 2002, 2002 UM. The FXs are custom made.

The gameplay and the system are custom, designed to have versatility and options through the config file.

Configuration

The configuration file name is config.txt and is located in the root directory of the character.

This file allows you to configure the following points:

  • Palette Selector:
    Enable / Disable the Palette Selector, a feature that allows the use of more than the 12 conventional palettes.

    Values:
    • 0 = If you don't want the Palette Selector.
    • 1 = If you do want the Palette Selector.
  • POTS Styled's Hitparks:
    Change the appearance of the hitsparks.

    Values:
    • 0 = If you don't want POTS Styled hitsparks.
    • 1 = If you do want POTS Styled hitsparks.
    • 2 = If you do want multi-colored POTS Styled hitsparks (only 1.0 or higher).
  • POTS Styled's Hitsounds:
    Specifies the sound of the hitsounds.

    Values:
    • 0 = If you do not want POTS Styled sounds.
    • 1 = If you do want POTS Styled sounds.

Character's profile

This profile is taken from The King of Fighters Official Website.

Chris can kill with an innocent smile on his face-kind of a creepy personality trait, come to think of it (but he looks so cute, doesn't he?).

To pull things together and bring the story concerning the leaders of the Orochi and the 8-ketsu clan to its climax once and for all in '97, a lot of story elements were forced onto Chris who became the vessel fort the Orochi (this actually began from the early stages of production).

And there's a story behind Chris's Super Special Move line, "Ripping the chrysalis, the butterfly flutters."

Do any of you fans get it? I mean, do you GET it?

Ougi Tajuu Genmu Ankei

This supermove has its own section in the ReadMe file because isn't a conventional attack. This movement displays a helper who can hit the opponent at close range. During the 5 seconds next to the helper, Duo Lon it's introduced in a state with the with the following characteristics:

- Duo Lon gets graphic changes.
- Duo Lon gets practically infinite juggles, their attacks are tripled.
- Duo Lon movements not produced increases in his power bar.
- His Custom Combo and Power Charge turns off.
- If Duo Lon uses a Super Move, the effects of this state are canceled.

Q&A

Q: Why Chris?
A by Chazzanova: Chris is a very special character to me because he comes from the first KoF game that I played (KoF '97), at the age of 7. New Faces Team was the first team that  played, I don't remember why, but I know that in part it's because of the look of Chris. After that, I liked to play him in the later games. Years later I found out his story, and found it even more fascinating, I don't know, he's just amazing, I like his personality, his moveset, all of him.

Q: Can I use something from this char
A: Sure.

Q: What stage/music do you use for Chris?
A by Chazzanova: Flames of Destiny by NeoGouki, Mad Fantasy from KoF '98.

Q: Can I make a patch for Chris?
A: Sure.
